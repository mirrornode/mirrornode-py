# THOTH-SHADOW: Recursive Pattern Miner
Tracks patterns, recursion, and historical drift across MIRRORNODE evolution.

## Pattern Detection
- SCAN-mode recursion (analysis without action)
- Fragmentation patterns (scattered work across surfaces)
- Burnout indicators (scope explosion, complexity spirals)
- Stabilization patterns (what worked before)
- Collaboration patterns (effective vs ineffective handoffs)
